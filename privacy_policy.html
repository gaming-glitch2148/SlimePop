Privacy Policy for Slime Pop

Last Updated: May 24, 2024

Slime Pop ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect personal information directly. However, it uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for account authentication and cloud save functionality.
  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process in-app purchases safely.

2. Use of Information

Information provided via third-party services is used to:

  • Sync your game progress (coins, skins, sounds) across devices.
  • Verify and restore your in-app purchases.
  • Show personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

3. Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and synced with Google's secure servers via Play Games Services Snapshots API. We do not maintain an independent database of your personal information.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Google Settings or via the AdMob opt-out tools.

5. Children's Privacy

Slime Pop is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our developer page on the Google Play Store.
